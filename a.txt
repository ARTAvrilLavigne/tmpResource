1.使用root账户登录服务器后台
2. 执行命令删除NetEcoIPowerSolService服务的定制信息，请注意 如下命令中的版本号 需要根据环境来看
rm /opt/share/oss/manager/DeployAgent/pubfiles/uniep.resource.NetEco.Product.apps/contents/NetEcoIPowerSolService-21.220.4/pub/auth/common_login_entry.json
3.依次执行如下命令：
rm -rf /opt/share/oss/manager/DeployAgent/svcs-pub/NetEco-Product-SMLogLicWebsite-smloglicwebsite
su ossadm -c "/opt/oss/manager/agent/bin/ipmc_adm -cmd restartapp -app SMLogLicWebsite"
rm -rf /opt/share/oss/manager/DeployAgent/svcs-pub/NetEco-Product-BasicWebsite-basicwebsite
su ossadm -c "/opt/oss/manager/agent/bin/ipmc_adm -cmd restartapp -app BasicWebsite"
4. 刷新登录页面
